# Contrast Testing

## Recommended Color Combinations

### Text on White Background

| Combination | Contrast | Level | Recommendation |
|-------------|----------|-------|----------------|
| Gray 900 on White | 18.4:1 | AAA | Headings |
| Gray 600 on White | 7.1:1 | AAA | Body text |
| Gray 500 on White | 4.7:1 | AA | Secondary text |
| Orange 700 on White | 4.6:1 | AA | Accent text, links |
| Orange 800 on White | 6.4:1 | AA+ | Strong accent text |

### Text on Orange Background

| Combination | Contrast | Level |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| White on Orange 500 | 3.1:1 | Large text only |
| White on Orange 600 | 3.6:1 | Large text only |
| White on Orange 700 | 4.6:1 | AA |

### Text on Dark Background

| Combination | Contrast | Level |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| White on Gray 900 | 18.4:1 | AAA |
| Gray 100 on Gray 900 | 16.2:1 | AAA |
| Orange 400 on Gray 900 | 6.2:1 | AA+ |

## Right vs. Wrong

### Right

- Text in **Orange 700** (#C2570A) on a white background
- Text in **Gray 600** (#52525B) as the default text color
- Orange 500 as a **button background** with white text (≥24px)
- Orange 500 as a **decorative accent** (lines, icons ≥24px)

### Wrong

- ❌ Text in Orange 500 (#FF8500) on a white background
- ❌ Orange 500 on Orange 50 for small text
- ❌ Gray 400 as body text color
- ❌ White on Orange 500 for text below 18px bold
