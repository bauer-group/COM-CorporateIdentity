## Email Signature

### Structure

```text
First Last
Position / Department
───────────────────
BAUER GROUP
T: +49 123 456789
E: first.last@bauer-group.com
W: bauer-group.com
```

### Rules

- Logo: BAUER GROUP wide variant, max. 200 px wide
- Font: System font (Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif)
- Colors: Orange (#FF8500) only for divider or logo
- No images, quotes or social media icons in the signature
- No custom background colors or banners

## Newsletter

### Planned Specifications

- Header with logo + issue number
- Maximum width: 600 px
- Inline CSS (no external stylesheet)
- Responsive layout (single column below 480 px)
- Footer with unsubscribe link and legal notice
