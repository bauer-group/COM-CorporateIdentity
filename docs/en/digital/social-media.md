## Design Rules

### Do's

- Always place logo in a corner (preferably bottom right)
- Ensure sufficient contrast between text and background
- Use consistent type hierarchy per platform
- Apply brand colors as accents

### Don'ts

- No distorted or cropped logos
- No text on busy backgrounds without overlay
- No inconsistent color palettes across platforms
- No stock photos without clearance
