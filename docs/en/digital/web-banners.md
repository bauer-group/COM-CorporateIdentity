## Banner Formats

| Type | Dimensions | Usage |
|------|------------|-------|
| Hero Banner | 1920 × 600 px | Website headers, landing pages |
| Open Graph Image | 1200 × 630 px | Link previews (social sharing) |
| Blog Header | 1200 × 400 px | Article images |
| Sidebar Banner | 300 × 250 px | Ad placements |
| Leaderboard | 728 × 90 px | Ad placements |

## Design Principles

- **Whitespace** — Keep at least 20% of the area clear
- **Logo placement** — Always visible, never centered on full-bleed images
- **CTA buttons** — In BAUER Orange (#FF8500) with white text
- **Text amount** — Maximum 2 headline lines + 1 subtext line
