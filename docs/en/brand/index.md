## Brand Personality

BAUER GROUP communicates:

| Trait | How we sound | How we do NOT sound |
|-------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Professional** | Factual, competent, clear | Academic, stiff, distant |
| **Pragmatic** | Solution-oriented, direct | Theoretical, bureaucratic |
| **Approachable** | Eye-level, partnership-driven | Crude, unprofessional, chummy |
| **Confident** | Calm, knowledgeable | Arrogant, exaggerated |

## Tone of Voice

### Do's

- **Active voice** ‚Äî "We develop" instead of "It was developed"
- **Clear statements** ‚Äî "This saves 40% cost" instead of "Savings could potentially be achieved"
- **Short sentences** ‚Äî One thought per sentence
- **Formal/informal by context** ‚Äî Technical docs: informal, Enterprise communications: formal

### Don'ts

- No superlatives without evidence ("world's best", "unique")
- No buzzwords without substance ("synergy", "paradigm shift")
- No passive constructions
- No unnecessary jargon at the expense of readability
