# Business Cards

Double-sided business cards for BAUER GROUP — the front in the primary language (German or Chinese), the back in English. Each card carries a personalized QR code linking to the employee's LinkStack profile.

## Language Variants

| Variant | Front | Back |
|---------|-------|------|
| **DE / EN** | German | English |
| **CN / EN** | Chinese | English |

:::

## Layout — Back (English)

Same layout as the front, all text in English (Germany, Phone, Managing Director, etc.).

## QR Code & LinkStack

Every business card carries a **personalized QR code** that points to the employee's individual LinkStack profile.

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Platform** | [LinkStack](https://linkstack.org/) (self-hosted) |
| **URL scheme** | `links.bauer-group.com/{firstname.lastname}` |
| **Example** | `links.bauer-group.com/karl.bauer` |
| **Placement** | Front (primary language) |

::: tip Personalization
The QR code is unique per employee. When ordering new business cards, the name must be specified in the URL scheme. The LinkStack profile is managed centrally and can be updated at any time — without reprinting the cards.
:::
