# Paper Recommendations

## Business Stationery

| Product | Weight | Paper Type | Note |
|---------|--------|------------|------|
| **Business cards** | 350 g/m² | Matte coated | Soft-touch lamination for premium feel |
| **Letterhead** | 100–120 g/m² | Offset, natural white | Optimal type rendering |
| **Envelopes** | 100 g/m² | Offset, natural white | Matches the letterhead |
| **Letterhead 2nd page** | 90 g/m² | Offset, natural white | Lighter paper for follow-up pages |

## Marketing Material

| Product | Weight | Paper Type | Note |
|---------|--------|------------|------|
| **Brochures (cover)** | 250–300 g/m² | Glossy art paper | Matte lamination optional |
| **Brochures (interior)** | 170 g/m² | Art paper | Gloss for photos, matte for text |
| **Flyers** | 170–250 g/m² | Art paper | Depending on use case |
| **Posters** | 170 g/m² | Glossy art paper | From A2 upward, 135 g/m² is also possible |

## Finishing

| Finishing | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Soft-touch lamination** | Business cards, folder covers |
| **UV spot varnish** | Logo accent on business cards |
| **Blind embossing** | Premium letterhead |
| **Hot foil stamping (orange)** | Special editions |

::: info Sustainability note
Prefer **FSC-certified** or **recycled paper** wherever print quality is not compromised. For image materials, high-grade virgin-fiber paper is acceptable.
:::
