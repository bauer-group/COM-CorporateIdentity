# Logo — Misuse

The following applications are **not permitted** and damage brand integrity.

## Do not stretch or distort

The logo must never be altered in its proportions — neither stretched nor compressed horizontally or vertically.

## Do not rotate

The logo must not be displayed rotated or tilted. The orientation is always horizontal.

## Do not recolor

The brand colors of the logo must not be changed. In particular, the orange icon must not be recolored to other colors.

::: danger Forbidden

- No gradients on the icon
- No blue, green, red or other colors
- No transparency reduction below 100%
:::

## Do not add effects

The logo must not be enhanced with effects:

- ❌ No shadows (drop shadow, box shadow)
- ❌ No outlines (outline, stroke)
- ❌ No 3D effects or bevel
- ❌ No blur
- ❌ No textures or patterns

## Not on busy backgrounds

The logo must not be placed on photographic backgrounds, patterns or textures that impair legibility.

::: tip Best Practice
When in doubt, use the logo on a white or solid dark background.
:::
